Lapwing

Northern Lapwing is an elegant bird. Highly migratory, this species is common throughout temperate regions of Eurasia. Adult male in breeding plumage has glossy green upperparts, including wing coverts and tertials. Scapulars show purple sheen. Primary flight feathers are buffy-white tipped. Rump is chestnut. Uppertail is white with broad subterminal black band and narrow white terminal line. Outer rectrices are entirely white.
